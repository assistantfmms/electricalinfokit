FAQs & Franchisee Stories

Do I need to be experienced?▼
No — all training is provided. You just need a good attitude, work ethic and communication skills.
Where can I work?▼
Wherever you want. Set up and adjust your own work zones anytime via your online profile.
Can I take time off?▼
It's your business — simply switch your status to "On Holidays" in your online portal.
What vehicle do I need?▼
Any new or used white van/cab chassis with a service body, no more than 5 years old (national fleet discounts available).
Can I add more vans?▼
Yes — expand with extra vans without paying additional franchise fees.
How long is the franchise term?▼
20 years, with the option to renew at no cost.
What if I want to sell?▼
Jim's Antennas & Security will assist with the sale and prepare the legal documentation.
Is there enough work?▼
Our team services around 4,000 jobs per month — consistent, year-round demand.
